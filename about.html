ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Ivan Oviedo Marin. I'm a junior developer looking to grow my skills in both programming and graphic design. My goal is to demonstrate my knowledge in either — or both — areas as needed, while bringing real value to any project I'm part of. I'm 20 years old and from Costa Rica. I hold a degree in Programming and Multimedia Technology from the University of Costa Rica (UCR), along with a Digital Marketing certificate from UAM. I have solid knowledge of Object-Oriented Programming (OOP), with experience in JavaScript, HTML, Python, and database management using Oracle, MySQL, and MongoDB, among other technologies. On the design side, I'm skilled in a wide range of tools, including Illustrator, Photoshop, Canva, and Figma, as well as drawing and editing applications such as Procreate, Krita, and MediBang, among many others. I'm passionate about drawing and consider myself a highly creative person, always bringing fresh ideas to every project. I'm also very attentive to instructions and detail, making sure every result matches exactly what's expected.
